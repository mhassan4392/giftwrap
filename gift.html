About Us

Creativity is making the complicated look simple

Ellen Arruda

Senior Designer

Fueled by her passion for design and colour coordination, Gifted Wraps creator, Ellen Arruda, calls on her vast experience in the packaging business to bring the best products and selection to her clients. With years of experience in the industry, Ellen is on top of packaging trends, options available in the market place, and accessories that add just the right amount of flair. Successfully blending her creativity with business sense, Gifted Wraps was launched to produce beautiful works of art in the form of wrapped gifts.

Wrapping a gift beautifully has always been one of her favourite pastimes and carefully selecting the perfect paper and ribbons gives Ellen as much pleasure as the recipient feels in discovering the contents inside her creation. It is Gifted Wraps' goal to ensure that each recipient of a specially wrapped package feels the effort that's gone into not only choosing the gift but in presenting it beautifully. Not everyone has the time, patience or resources to present exquisitely wrapped gifts, that's where Gifted Wraps comes in.

"Presenting a beautifully wrapped gift to someone shows them just how much you care, and presentation is everything, it creates the excitement! Just like when you were a child and given a present, I wanted to know what was inside the lovely package but I wanted to savour the moment." ~ Ellen Arruda
